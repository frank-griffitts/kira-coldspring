DUNGEON MASTERS ONLY

Location: Belladonna - The Rangers Guild House

Exterior Description

The Rangers Guild House stands half-hidden beneath the tall pines on the north edge of Belladonna. Crafted from mossy greenstone and dark weathered timber, it blends into the forest like a predator in ambush. A slanted slate roof is overgrown with ivy and hanging moss. A large hawk-carved crest over the main archway marks the entrance — the symbol of the Rangers Guild. Thick vines wrap around the building’s outer beams, and small blue foxgloves grow wild in the overgrown garden beds. Nearby, a training field with archery targets and wooden dummies is patrolled by young initiates.

Interior Description

  • Entry Hall: A dimly lit chamber with stone floors and wooden rafters. Animal pelts hang as tapestries. A massive fireplace crackles in winter. Trophy heads of fantastical beasts are mounted on the walls. A notice board near the door is filled with bounty slips and scouting reports.
  • War Room: A round chamber with a stone table etched with a map of the Kingdom of Yew. Miniature tokens track movements of threats and patrols. Arrows, parchment scrolls, and bottles of ink clutter the shelves.
  • The Armory: Rows of bows, crossbows, throwing knives, and green-cloaked uniforms hang neatly. Special racks hold enchanted arrows and rare alchemical gear. Hidden compartment under floorboards contains poisons and contraband collected from outlaws.
  • Warden’s Quarters: Spartan yet organized: a modest bed, leather-bound journals, a locked chest, and an oil painting of a young ranger team — one figure blacked out with ink.
  • Barracks: Simple cots and personal lockers. Smells faintly of pine oil and worn leather. A hidden trapdoor beneath one cot leads to the secret tunnel.
  • Training Hall: Open-air hall with retractable roof slats. Wooden dummies enchanted to fight back. A high perch is used to train hawks and ravens for message delivery.

Backstory

Rellen is actually Elira Hawthorn, niece of King Garrius Hawthorn. As a young prodigy of the wilds, she joined the Rangers Guild to escape court politics. Her squad was ambushed in the Blackpines Thicket, a betrayal linked to Minister Varis. She now hides her identity and seeks the truth behind the conspiracy, all while rebuilding the Guild. Her encrypted journal holds dangerous knowledge.

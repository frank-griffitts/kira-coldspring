DUNGEON MASTERS ONLY

Location: Hemlock Caverns - The Catacombs of Echoes

Entrance & Overview

Directly beneath the Forgotten Reliquary, the Catacombs of Echoes are reached via a spiraling descent through the Ember Crucible. The air grows fetid and warm, thick with mildew and rot. Sound behaves strangely—whispers echo too long, and footsteps rebound with ghostly persistence.

Chambers of the Catacombs

  • Ossuary of the Lost Choir: Cracked bone shelves line the walls, each skull etched with a rune. DC 15 Religion: Moonspire chanters who died protecting the wards. A discordant sound awakens 1d4 Bone Chanters. A chewed tunnel leads west into the scorpion nest.
  • The Whispering Hollow: A ruined chapel with a broken obsidian altar. Vines twitch despite still air. All hear whispering in Abyssal. DC 13 Wisdom Save: Hallucination of being buried alive. Inside the altar: 🗡 Dagger of Quiet Mercy — +1 weapon, +1d4 psychic damage if wielder is silent that round.
  • The Scorpion Nest: Clay tunnels reinforced with chitin and bone. Dozens of pale eggs. Guarded by 2 Giant Broodmothers and the Scorpion Husk Matron. Corpses of adventurers litter the nest, one clutching a corrupted Trade Ministry sigil.

Backstory

Once a burial site for Moonspire clergy, the catacombs were corrupted when a Trade Ministry summoner—secretly a cultist of Varis—botched a ritual. The summoning infused local scorpions with infernal essence and weakened the veil between this world and the Demonrealm of Yaldaboath. Now the tainted creatures tunnel toward the Dark Gate, acting as its sentinels.

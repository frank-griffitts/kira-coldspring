NPC: Rellen Thornwatch – Warden of Belladonna (Secretly Elira Hawthorn)

Basic Information

  • Race: Half-Elf
  • Class/Levels: Ranger (Level 6, Gloom Stalker – Xanathar’s Guide)
  • Background: Outlaw Protector (Modified Folk Hero)
  • Alignment: Chaotic Good
  • Age: 40
  • Gender: Female
  • Height: 5’9”
  • Eyes: Hazel, wary
  • Hair: Long chestnut with streaks of gray
  • Skin: Olive-toned with old scar on her jaw
  • Role: Secret Ally, Ranger Warden

Combat Statistics

  • Armor Class (AC): 17 (Studded Leather + Dex)

  • Saving Throws: Strength +4, Dexterity +7
  • Skills: Stealth +7, Survival +6, Insight +6, Perception +6, Nature +3, Animal Handling +6
  • Damage Resistances: —
  • Condition Immunities: —
  • Senses: Darkvision 60 ft, Passive Perception 16
  • Languages: Common, Elvish, Sylvan, Thieves’ Cant
  • Challenge Rating (CR): —

Special Traits & Features

  • Favored Enemy – Humanoids (Dark Elves, Ministers of Trade): Advantage on Survival to track; learn one language (Thieves’ Cant)
  • Natural Explorer – Forest: Double proficiency for tracking/survival; group stealth/travel bonuses
  • Fighting Style – Archery: +2 to ranged weapon attack rolls
  • Dread Ambusher (Lvl 3):
    • +10 ft speed on first turn
    • One extra weapon attack
    • That attack deals +1d8 damage
  • Umbral Sight: Darkvision 60 ft; invisible to creatures relying on darkvision in darkness
  • Extra Attack: Attack twice per Attack action

Spellcasting

  • Spellcasting Ability: Wisdom (DC 13, +5 to hit)
  • Spell Slots: 3 (1st), 2 (2nd)
  • Prepared Spells:
    • Cantrip (Always Prepared): Disguise Self
    • 1st Level: Hunter’s Mark, Speak with Animals, Cure Wounds
    • 2nd Level: Pass Without Trace, Silence, Darkvision

Equipment

  • Thornpiercer: +1 longbow made of ancient Blackpine; once per day, ignore all cover and add +1d8 if hidden; regenerates 1d4 arrows/hr in forest (max 40)
  • Dagger with emerald pommel: Family heirloom
  • Quiver (40 arrows)
  • Herbalist pouch
  • Disguise kit
  • Royal seal ring (hidden in boot)
  • Torn ranger tabard (often reburied or burned)

Personality & Backstory

  • Trait: "I walk the edge of light and shadow. Most people never notice."
  • Ideal: "I protect people not for glory—but because someone must."
  • Bond: "My cousin wears a crown in a court of lies. I will unmask the serpent beside her."
  • Flaw: "I cannot forgive myself for surviving when my squad did not."
  • Player's Goal: Protect Belladonna in secret, expose Varis, and reveal identity to Princess Hawthorn.
  • Background Summary: Once a noble of House Hawthorn, Elira became a ranger to escape court life. After her squad was betrayed and slaughtered 15 years ago, she faked her death and assumed the identity of Rellen Thornwatch. Now a respected Warden in Belladonna, only the dwarf sage Onyx suspects her true identity.

Relationships

  • Princess Hawthorn: Secret cousin, protected from afar
  • Onyx: Trusted confidant, may know her true identity
  • Kira Coldspring: Rising ranger she deeply believes in, but fears will follow Jorsh’s doomed path
  • Baider: Fellow ranger; shares tough love and grudging respect
  • Elias: Feels a protective, almost maternal pull toward him due to a promise made

Locations Associated

  • Blackpines Thicket: Site of ambush and betrayal; her squad perished here
  • Rangers Guild House: Current secret post, operates as Warden
  • Royal Castle of Yew: Childhood home; estranged by duty and deception

Roleplaying Notes

  • Speaks plainly and acts decisively; no patience for noble ceremony
  • Refers to herself in third person when slipping into her Rellen identity
  • Dry, sometimes dark sense of humor, especially under stress
  • Believes Kira could be the future—but also fears she will repeat her own mistakes

Plot Hooks

  • Rellen can send the party to investigate signs of renewed activity in Blackpines Thicket
  • She may offer the use of Thornpiercer to a trusted PC for a limited time
  • Eventually reveals her identity to Princess Hawthorn—or asks the party to do it for her
  • Knows ancient secrets about Varis' pact with the Ministry of Trade and its roots in shadow magic

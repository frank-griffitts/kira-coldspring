DUNGEON MASTERS ONLY

Location: The Blackpines Thicket – Abandoned Ranger Outpost

Exterior Description

Deep in the twisted woodland northeast of Belladonna, shrouded in near-perpetual mist and dead silence, the Blackpines Thicket is a cursed wood. The outpost is a moss-covered stone structure, built into a hillside and nearly invisible from above. Its watchtower has long since collapsed, and thorny blackpine roots overgrow the entire roof. Arcana (DC 14) or Nature (DC 16) reveals: the place is tainted by a suppressed blood-curse. A scratched Ranger’s sigil on the stone door is overwritten with hardened black sap, spiraling like glass.

Interior Description

  • Main Hall / Barracks: Caved-in roof, shattered furniture, and bloody footprints under the dust. Investigation (DC 13) reveals a rusted dagger with Elira’s sigil. Arcana (DC 15) detects shadow magic not used by Rangers.
  • Command Room: A smashed war table with torn maps. A hidden compartment hides a coded report from Elira, warning: “Two men wear the same face. We are being set up.” DC 16 Investigation or Linguistics to decode.
  • Armory (Collapsed): A false wall conceals:
    • 2x +1 Ranger Shortswords
    • Elira’s Hunting Cloak (advantage on Stealth in forests, smells of smoke and elderberry)
  • Ritual Circle Room (Secret Basement): Found under a cracked training dummy. Ancient stone circle, corrupted for shadowmancy. Arcana (DC 17): evidence of summoning or binding during the ambush.

Stat Blocks

Veyna Greaves (Ghost, CR 2 Variant)
AC: 12 | HP: 45 | Speed: 0 ft. (hover)
Haunting Memory: One PC relives betrayal (WIS save DC 15 or Frightened for 1 min)
Possession: Only triggers if a PC lies or withholds info about Varis.
Rest Condition: Spirit laid to rest through truth, justice, or sacred rites.

Backstory

Elira Hawthorn led a squad into the Thicket, following a false report orchestrated by Minister Varis. It was a trap. Varis, newly aligned with the Shadow Court, tested soulburn rituals and shadow clones during the massacre. Elira witnessed Varis’s true form and fled, choosing to remain hidden. The Ministry used the event to discredit the Rangers and increase their control of Belladonna.

NPC: Baider – Veteran Fighter and Kira’s Guardian

Basic Information

  • Race: Half-Orc
  • Class/Levels: Fighter (Level 5, Champion Archetype)
  • Background: Mercenary Veteran
  • Alignment: True Neutral
  • Age: 48
  • Gender: Male
  • Height: 6’5”
  • Eyes: Yellow
  • Hair: Black, streaked with gray
  • Skin: Weathered gray-green with numerous battle scars
  • Role: Gruff Mentor / Ally

Special Traits & Features

  • Second Wind (1/rest): Regain 1d10 + 5 HP as a bonus action.
  • Action Surge (1/rest): Take one additional action on your turn.
  • Extra Attack: Attack twice when you take the Attack action.
  • Fighting Style – Defense: +1 AC while wearing armor.
  • Improved Critical (Champion): Score a critical hit on a roll of 19–20.

Spellcasting

None

Equipment

  • Sootfang: +1 heavy battleaxe made from black steel with a scorched rune. Once per long rest, can cast Compelled Duel on hit. On a crit, deals +1d6 fire damage.
  • Steel shield with faded sigil of the Eastern Border Guard
  • Chainmail armor
  • Smith’s hammer (functions as a light melee weapon)
  • Belt pouch containing scraps of Kira’s old training blade
  • Cracked iron coin from Onyx
  • Empty flask of orcish firebrandy (sentimental)

Personality & Backstory

  • Trait: “I speak with my hands. Usually when they’re holding a hammer.”
  • Ideal: “Strength means protecting those who can’t protect themselves—even if they hate you for it.”
  • Bond: “Kira was the only good thing I ever forged. And I broke her.”
  • Flaw: “I don’t ask for help. I make demands or I leave.”
  • Player's Goal: To reconcile with Kira, bury old regrets, and protect Belladonna from the dark tide rising from Hemlock Caverns.
  • Background Summary: Baider fought in the Border Wars as a mercenary, forging bonds with adventurers like Onyx the dwarven healer. After a catastrophic delve into Hemlock Caverns, Baider returned to Belladonna where he found a lone half-elf toddler in the ashes of a burned caravan. He raised her—Kira—as if forging steel. But his rough hands and soldier’s mind left scars. She fled him, and now, as darkness rises again, he seeks a second chance.

Relationships

  • Kira Coldspring: Adoptive daughter; relationship is strained and unresolved.
  • Onyx the Sage: Former adventuring companion; bond broken after Hemlock Caverns.
  • Jorsh: Former friend of Kira, betrayed Baider’s trust and the village of Belladonna.

Locations Associated

  • Belladonna: Resides in the village as a blacksmith and war veteran.
  • Hemlock Caverns: Site of past trauma and a failed expedition with Onyx.
  • Baider’s Forge: Contains a secret tunnel to the catacombs and a shrine to fallen comrades.

Roleplaying Notes

  • Gruff but fiercely loyal; actions speak louder than words.
  • Uses blunt, terse dialogue; often dry or bitterly humorous.
  • Keeps a war memorial to fallen friends in a hidden alcove.
  • Sharpens weapons to distract from emotional pain.
  • Suspicious of magic and fate—especially after Onyx’s “visions.”

Plot Hooks

  • Recruits the party to investigate strange noises beneath the forge — which lead to ancient catacombs.
  • Needs help tracking Kira or Jorsh after a key betrayal or revelation.
  • Holds a relic from Hemlock Caverns that is key to unlocking a divine ward or seal.
  • Can craft a unique magic weapon — but only after confronting his own past in the caverns again.

NPC: Kira Coldspring – Level 5 Ranger (Gloom Stalker)

  • Race: Half-Elf
  • Class/Levels: Ranger (Gloom Stalker Archetype – Xanathar’s Guide to Everything)

  • Age: 20
  • Gender: Female
  • Height: 5’7”
  • Eyes: Green
  • Hair: Short, black
  • Skin: Pale with ash freckles
  • Role: Protagonist, Shadow Hunter, Tracker

Special Traits & Features

  • Favored Enemy (Dark Elves & Cultists): Advantage on Survival checks to track, and Intelligence checks to recall info.
  • Natural Explorer (Forest): Double proficiency on Int/Wis checks; move stealthily at normal pace.
  • Dread Ambusher: +Wis to initiative (+6); on first turn: +10 ft movement and one extra weapon attack (+1d8 dmg).
  • Umbral Sight: Darkvision to 90 ft; invisible to creatures relying on darkvision in darkness.
  • Gloom Stalker Magic: Disguise Self, Rope Trick always prepared.

  • Spell Slots: 4 (1st), 2 (2nd)

    • 2nd Level: Rope Trick, Pass without Trace

Equipment

  • Whisperfang: Magical longbow (+1, counts as magical, casts Hunter’s Mark 1/day)
  • Short sword (silvered)
  • Studded leather armor
  • Cloak of Elvenkind (advantage on Stealth; creatures have disadvantage to detect)
  • 2 healing potions
  • Herbalist kit
  • Small locket with sketch of Jorsh (burned, repaired)
  • Journal of Shadow Realm clues and sightings

Personality & Backstory

  • Trait: “I don’t talk about my feelings—I track them, then bury them.”
  • Ideal: “No one else should have to grow up the way I did.”
  • Bond: “Jorsh betrayed me… or was he betrayed first?”
  • Flaw: “I push people away so they won’t leave me.”
  • Player’s Goal: To find and confront Jorsh, learn the truth of her past, and protect Belladonna from the rising darkness.
  • Background Summary: Kira was raised by Baider, a brutal but loyal half-orc. Her bond with Jorsh was her first warmth—his fall left her scarred. Now a skilled tracker, she patrols the wilds around Belladonna, hunting shadows and secrets.

Relationships

  • Baider: Harsh adoptive guardian; their relationship is strained but rooted in a painful loyalty.
  • Jorsh: Former love; his betrayal drives her forward even as she questions what truly happened.
  • Elias: Fellow ranger and friend, secretly in love with Kira and often her voice of reason.

Locations Associated

  • Belladonna: Her home base; a small township she’s sworn to protect.
  • Hemlock Caverns: Believed to be a key to Jorsh’s plans and ancient secrets.
  • Realm of Shadows: The source of growing darkness; Kira maps and tracks sightings obsessively.

Roleplaying Notes

  • Speaks plainly with dry sarcasm.
  • Never fully trusts authority figures.
  • Steps up reluctantly when no one else will.
  • Haunted by guilt about Jorsh—wants closure, not just revenge.
  • Keeps a private, hand-annotated map tied to the Realm of Shadows.

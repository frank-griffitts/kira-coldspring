NPC: Jorsh Sablevein – The Betrayer

Basic Information

  • Race: Human (Quarter-Elf)
  • Class/Levels: Sorcerer (Shadow) 7 / Warlock (Raven Queen) 4
  • Background: Exile
  • Alignment: Chaotic Neutral (flirting with Chaotic Evil)
  • Role: Antagonist, tragic antihero, dark sorcerer
  • Challenge Rating: 9 (5,000 XP)

Ability Scores

AbilityScoreModifier
Strength10+0
Dexterity14+2
Constitution18+4
Intelligence16+3
Wisdom12+1
Charisma20+5

Combat Statistics

  • Armor Class (AC): 16 (Mage Armor)
  • Hit Points (HP): 112 (11d6 + 44)
  • Speed: 30 ft.
  • Proficiency Bonus: +4
  • Saving Throws: Con +8, Cha +9, Dex +6
  • Skills: Arcana +8, Insight +5, Deception +9, Persuasion +8, Stealth +6
  • Damage Resistances: Necrotic, Psychic
  • Condition Immunities: Charmed, Frightened (while in dim light or darkness)
  • Senses: Darkvision 120 ft, Passive Perception 14
  • Languages: Common, Elvish, Deep Speech

Special Traits & Features

  • Darkvision: 120 ft.
  • Fey Ancestry: Advantage vs. being charmed; magic can’t put him to sleep.
  • Eyes of the Dark: See normally in all darkness.
  • Strength of the Grave: Once per long rest, drop to 1 HP instead of 0 (Charisma save DC 15).
  • Metamagic: Twinned Spell, Subtle Spell
  • Invocations: Agonizing Blast, One with Shadows

Spellcasting

  • Spellcasting Ability: Charisma (DC 17, +9 to hit)
  • Spell Slots: Warlock (2 slots at 2nd level)
  • Cantrips: Prestidigitation, Eldritch Blast, Mage Hand, Minor Illusion, Chill Touch
  • Warlock Spells: Misty Step, Hex, Mirror Image, Hold Person
  • Sorcerer Spells: Mage Armor, Counterspell, Shadow Blade, Greater Invisibility, Fireball, Suggestion, Darkness, Silence

Actions

  • Multiattack: Casts a spell and makes one Eldritch Blast (2 beams)
  • Eldritch Blast: +9 to hit, 120 ft., 1d10+5 force damage per beam
  • Shadow Blade: +8 to hit, 2d8 psychic damage, advantage in dim light (Concentration)

Legendary Actions

  • Flicker Step (1): Teleport 30 ft. in dim light or shadows
  • Whisper of Dread (1): One creature within 10 ft. must succeed on a DC 17 Wisdom save or be frightened

Personality & Backstory

  • Ideal: "I must never be powerless again."
  • Bond: "Kira is the only thing left that matters. Even if she hates me."
  • Flaw: "I believe only I can fix what’s broken—no one else understands."
  • Background Summary: Jorsh was a promising boy turned tragic fugitive after the betrayal of his family by Varis. Now a powerful sorcerer, he walks the line between redemption and destruction, tethered to Kira by love and grief.

Relationships

  • Kira Coldspring: Childhood friend and secret love. Still deeply connected.
  • Baider: Former father-figure who rejected him.
  • Onyx: A wary mentor who once saved him from falling too far.

Locations Associated

  • Hemlock Caverns: Where he found the grimoire and began his transformation.
  • Belladonna: His childhood home and site of betrayal.

Roleplaying Notes

  • Bitterness hides a deep wound of betrayal and loss.
  • He believes his cause is just, even if his methods are harsh.
  • Uses charm and manipulation as easily as shadows and spells.

Plot Hooks

  • Prophecies paint him as either savior or destroyer — which becomes true depends on player choices.
  • His knowledge of Varis and the dark elf conspiracy may be key to saving the realm.
  • He may offer uneasy alliance or tragic opposition depending on player moral alignment.

DUNGEON MASTERS ONLY

Location: Draconis Incubatorium – Caverns of Hemlock

Exterior Description

Deep beneath the Catacombs of Echoes, a scorched spiral stairwell descends into a vaulted basalt chamber. At its base stands a massive obsidian gate adorned with petrified dragon claws. Glyphs pulse with sickly green energy, guarding the sanctum.

Inscription above the gate (in Draconic and Infernal):
“Their wings shall rise in silence. Speak the name of the First Flame, or feed the nest in pieces.”

To open the gate, players must:

  • Speak “Valamandrix” — the true name of the progenitor dragon (lore from the Temple of the Moonspire)
  • Offer dragonborn blood willingly (DC 16 Persuasion)
  • Cast Greater Restoration or Dispel Evil and Good to suppress the glyphs (1 min)
Inside, dim veins of molten amethyst line the walls — dimming further when undead approach.

Interior Description

  • Cradle of Scales: Broken shells and twisted bones litter the floor. A lingering truth-ward causes pain when lying (1 psychic damage). Hidden beneath cracked banners, a single egg ticks like a heartbeat (DC 17 Investigation reveals it’s alive).
  • Gallery of Flame-Callers: Blackened murals once used to train young dragons. Puzzle: Rotate 4 dragon reliefs to match mural (DC 15 History) to reveal stair to the Magma Heart. Failure triggers a Flameborn Reaver from floor grates.
  • Nesting Core: A central cavern of runed nests and twitching undead hatchlings. At the far end, a skeletal dragon skull cradles the Chalice of Ash — pulsing necrotic power every 2 rounds (heals undead 1d8). Can be sealed with Hallow or 3 DC 15 Religion divine energy channelings.

Stat Blocks

Droxion, Warden of the Shattered Brood (Undead Dragonborn Warlock, CR 6)
AC: 17 | HP: 95 | Speed: 30 ft.
Resistances: Necrotic, Fire, Radiant
Abilities:

  • Voice of Ash (1/day): DC 15 WIS or be frightened and silenced for 1 minute
  • Necrotic Pulse (Recharge 5–6): 30 ft radius, 5d10 necrotic, DC 15 CON for half
  • Broodlink: Commands all undead wyrmlings on his initiative
  • Black Oath Blade: +7 to hit, 2d6 slashing + 2d8 necrotic; WIS DC 14 or frightened if below half HP
Tactics: Warns players to leave. Fights with wyrmlings if they persist. Attempts to drag a PC into the Chalice.

Backstory

Once guardian of the Incubatorium and loyal to Valamandrix, Droxion was born under the Moon Eclipse. Deceived by Yaldaboath during the Shadowfall, he sacrificed the last clutch of dragon eggs in return for their supposed safety.

Twisted by necrotic guilt, he now believes undeath may redeem the brood and bring immortality. If shown the Living Egg from the Cradle of Scales, he may hesitate, torn between sorrow and purpose.

Side Quest

“Ashes of the First Flame”
Trigger: Discovering the Living Egg or surviving Droxion’s plea
Objective:

  • Recover the Living Egg
  • Bring it to the Temple of the Moonspire for purification
  • Defeat or redeem Droxion
Reward:
  • Moon-Blessed Hatchling: A bonded young metallic dragon companion
  • Permanent +1 to necrotic saves from draconic purity
  • Story hook for Valamandrix’s future return

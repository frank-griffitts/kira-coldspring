Combat Statistics

Special Traits & Features

  • Whispering Grin (At-Will): Can cast Dissonant Whispers (3d6 psychic, DC 13 Wisdom save) at will. The victim hears their most shameful memory in Yithra’s voice.
  • Truth Bleed (1/Day): One creature within 30 ft. must succeed on a DC 14 Charisma save or involuntarily reveal a painful truth and have disadvantage on their next attack roll or saving throw.
  • False Hospitality: If a creature partakes in food or drink served by Yithra, they must make a DC 13 Wisdom save or be Charmed for 1 hour, treating Yithra as trustworthy and harmless.
  • Mogthul’s Ear: May cast Detect Thoughts once per short rest. She may ask one question aloud that targets a suppressed memory.

Spellcasting

  • Spellcasting Ability: Charisma (DC 13, +5 to hit)
  • Spell Slots: 2 (1st), 2 (2nd), 1 (3rd)
  • Cantrips: Prestidigitation, Eldritch Blast, Minor Illusion
  • 1st Level: Charm Person, Dissonant Whispers
  • 2nd Level: Detect Thoughts, Hold Person
  • 3rd Level: Major Image

Equipment

  • Ceremonial butcher’s apron, spattered with dried herbs and blood
  • Sigil of Mogthul (hidden inside her collar)
  • Jar of Whisperfat (renders secrets edible – non-magical but unsettling)
  • Living Grimoire (hidden under floorboards)

Personality & Backstory

  • Trait: "Cryptically nurturing, like a sharp-tongued grandmother who may feed you stew or summon your worst memory."
  • Ideal: "Secrets are nourishment. Lies are currency."
  • Bond: "She seeks the memory of a forbidden ritual erased from the royal archives."
  • Flaw: "She takes great joy in dismantling others' façades, even when it leads to chaos."
  • Player's Goal: May attempt to manipulate the party into retrieving a hidden memory artifact from the royal crypts.
  • Background Summary: Yithra is an exiled dark elf who communes with Mogthul the Listener Beneath. She walks a line between terrifying oracle and unsettling host, feeding off secrets and sowing doubt. She once served as a midwife for royal scandals and now plots to awaken Mogthul by harvesting dangerous truths.

Relationships

  • Minister Varis: Knows dark truths about him and hints at past associations
  • Kira Coldspring: Claims to know secrets about her bloodline and dreams

Locations Associated

  • Creosote Hollow: Locals avoid her hut, where whispers echo after dark
  • Moonspire Temple Ruins: Believes something was buried here by the clergy long ago

Roleplaying Notes

  • Use a sing-song, knowing voice, like someone enjoying a story no one else knows the ending to
  • Often stirs tea or cooks while revealing disturbing facts
  • Always pushes characters to reveal secrets—voluntarily or otherwise

  • Yithra requests the players retrieve a memory locked in a cursed artifact buried beneath the Moonspire.

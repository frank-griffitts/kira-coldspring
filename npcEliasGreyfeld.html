Personality & Backstory

  • Trait: "I speak only when I must, but I see what others miss."
  • Ideal: "The strong protect the vulnerable—even from the law, if need be."
  • Bond: "Kira gave me a reason to return to the Rangers. I will not lose her."
  • Flaw: "I don’t trust easily, and I often act alone when I should ask for help."
  • Player's Goal: Protect Kira, uncover the truth behind Jorsh and the Trade Ministry, and confront his own feelings.
  • Background Summary: Elias was once a sentinel of the northern border, a skilled ranger who lost his squad in an ambush. Wandering and broken, he was called back to Belladonna under Elira’s command. He sees himself as Kira’s protector, though his feelings for her run deeper than duty. Elias believes Jorsh’s betrayal is far more sinister than anyone suspects, and he fears for Kira’s heart and soul in the battles to come.

Relationships

  • Kira Coldspring: Trusted companion and hidden love interest; he feels fiercely protective of her
  • Elira (aka Rellen Thornwatch): Recruited Elias back to the Guild; Elias remains wary of her hidden motives

Locations Associated

  • Belladonna Rangers Guild: Second-in-command under Elira
  • Blackpine Thicket: Lived in seclusion after losing his squad
  • Hemlock Region: Mapping and patrolling area for signs of corruption

Roleplaying Notes

  • Speaks in short, clipped phrases—his words matter
  • Has a keen tactical mind and is rarely surprised
  • Feels uneasy about Elira’s past and the politics around the Trade Ministry
  • Emotionally reserved, but loyal to the bone

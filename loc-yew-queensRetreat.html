DUNGEON MASTERS ONLY

Location: Western Hemlock Wilderness – Hawthorn’s Retreat

Exterior Description

Hidden an hour’s hike west of Hemlock Falls through thistle-thick trails and moss-choked woods, Hawthorn’s Retreat appears at first like a ruin swallowed by the forest. A wooden gate set into a hill, veiled by tangled ivy and druidic illusions, conceals a fortified hollow of timber lodges and watch platforms camouflaged among tall pines. Above the entrance is a sigil carved into petrified bark—a stag entwined with thorns, the personal seal of House Hawthorn in exile.

To enter unseen, players must:

  • Succeed on a DC 16 Survival or Nature check to find the camouflaged trail
  • —or— possess a druidic token given by a ranger of the crown
Once inside, players find a perimeter marked by thorn-woven fences, camouflaged bunkers, and elevated platforms with hidden archers watching silently from the canopy.

Interior Description

  • The Stagfire Hall: A round timber structure with a central firepit and stone hearth shaped like a stag skull. Used as mess hall, strategy room, and command post. Maps, encrypted reports, and shadowy portraits of known enemies hang behind curtains. Only Princess Hawthorn may sit on the antlerwood throne without consequence.
  • The Watch Grove: A circle of standing stones within a hidden glade, maintained by Liora. Emits faint hums under moonlight. Allows up to 3 scrying rituals per night. Moon-touched flora bloom unnaturally here.
  • Ranger Barracks: Narrow tree-bound bunkhouses and hammocks. Spartan and efficient. Dardick’s room contains a locked blackwood chest with ashes from a forbidden pyre.
  • The Thornepath Tunnel: A sealed passage beneath the outpost, accessible only by Princess Hawthorn or Liora. Leads to an ancient elven causeway heading toward Hemlock Caverns' upper plateau.

Stat Blocks

Dardick Hawk (Half-Elf Ranger 6 – Gloom Stalker Archetype, CR 5)
AC: 16 | HP: 58 | Speed: 35 ft.
STR 13 | DEX 18 | CON 14 | INT 10 | WIS 15 | CHA 9
Skills: Stealth +8, Perception +5, Survival +6
Tools: Poisoner’s kit, Woodcarver’s tools
Senses: Darkvision 60 ft., Favored Terrain: Forest
Abilities:

  • Dread Ambusher: Extra attack in the first round of combat
  • Umbral Step (1/day): Become invisible for 1 minute while in dim light or darkness
  • Oathburned Mark (Secret): Once per long rest, can summon shadowy tendrils to restrain (DC 14 STR) a foe for 1 round. Sign of a pact with a forgotten archon.

Backstory

Once a loyal ranger of the Crown, Dardick led a doomed expedition into Hemlock Caverns and returned as the sole survivor. He made a pact with a forgotten archon to save his men—but failed. Now marked by shadow and bound by guilt, he serves Princess Hawthorn in silence, hoping to redeem himself or fall before the corruption consumes him. Dardick avoids mirrors, sleeps little, and carves small wooden animals which he burns during full moons.

NPC: Varis "Darknettle" Aelth'Dor – Minister of Trade | Secret Herald of the Deep Court

Basic Information

  • Race: Dark Elf
  • Class/Levels: Sorcerer/Warlock (Level 10)
  • Background: Prince in Exile
  • Alignment: Lawful Evil
  • Age: Unknown (appears early 30s)
  • Gender: Male
  • Height: 6'0"
  • Eyes: Violet, faintly glowing
  • Hair: Silken white, shoulder-length
  • Skin: Dusky grey with silver undertones
  • Role: Antagonist, Politician, Cult Leader

  • Languages: Common, Elvish, Undercommon, Infernal

Special Traits & Features

  • Fey Ancestry: Advantage on saving throws against being charmed; immune to magical sleep.
  • Shadow Step (Recharge 5–6): Teleport up to 30 ft in dim light or darkness.
  • Dark Elf Magic: Dancing Lights, Mage Hand (at will); Darkness, Faerie Fire (1/day each).
  • Master Manipulator: Once per long rest, impose disadvantage on any Insight check made against him.

Spellcasting

  • Spellcasting Ability: Charisma (DC 17, +9 to hit)
  • Spell Slots: 4 (1st), 3 (2nd), 3 (3rd), 3 (4th), 2 (5th)
  • Cantrips: Prestidigitation, Minor Illusion, Eldritch Blast, Chill Touch
  • 1st Level: Shield, Mage Armor, Charm Person
  • 2nd Level: Mirror Image, Suggestion
  • 3rd Level: Counterspell, Dispel Magic, Fly
  • 4th Level: Greater Invisibility, Phantasmal Killer
  • 5th Level: Dominate Person, Wall of Force

Equipment

  • Shadowfang Dagger – ceremonial dagger, +8 to hit, 1d6+4 piercing + 3d6 necrotic
  • Obsidian-thread robes with silver runes
  • Forged relic collection, including fake crowns and ritual masks

Personality & Backstory

  • Trait: "I speak in debts and promises. All else is noise."
  • Ideal: "Power is not given. It is taken."
  • Bond: "My fallen House will rise again. I will see it done."
  • Flaw: "I underestimate hope — especially in others."
  • Player's Goal: Unravel his plot and stop the awakening of Yaldaboath.
  • Background Summary: A prince in exile, master manipulator, secret herald of an ancient demonic court hidden beneath the Hemlock Caverns. Posing as a Minister, Varis seeks to open the Shadow Gate and restore the fallen Drow kingdom through sacrifice and deception.

Relationships

  • Kira Coldspring: Sees her as the key to awakening his god.
  • Princess Hawthorn: Holds blackmail over her family.
  • Jorsh the Betrayer: Former pawn, now discarded.
  • Onyx: A broken guardian whose failure benefited Varis.

Locations Associated

  • Belladonna: Controls merchant guilds and secret cultists
  • Hemlock Caverns: Site of Shadow Gate and ancient pact
  • Royal Court of Yew: Serves as Minister of Trade

Roleplaying Notes

  • Never raises his voice; always polite, even while threatening
  • Prefers favors and leverage to violence
  • Collects forgeries; fascinated by beautiful lies

Plot Hooks

  • Varis manipulates a noble NPC the party trusts, forcing them to choose sides.
  • A forged relic in his collection holds a real secret; uncovering it could turn the tide.
  • His plan to sacrifice Kira can be stopped — but only by sealing the Shadow Gate or negotiating with a worse evil.

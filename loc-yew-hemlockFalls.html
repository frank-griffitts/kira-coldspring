DUNGEON MASTERS ONLY

Location: Hemlock Falls & Entrance to Hemlock Caverns

Exterior Description

The Hemlock Falls cascade from a cliff of blackstone, crashing down into a mist-veiled basin surrounded by jagged rocks and twisted hemlock trees. The water is unnaturally clear—almost silver in moonlight—but bitterly cold to the touch. During full moons, ghostly figures can be seen reflected in the water that are not present in the material world. Behind the waterfall, barely visible unless closely inspected (DC 16 Perception), lies a moss-covered arch of obsidian stone, carved with forgotten sigils and ringed in chalk-like mineral deposits. The sound of the falls mutes all conversation within 15 feet, granting a sense of both awe and dread. Birds avoid the basin. Animals refuse to drink from it.

Interior Description

  • Echoing Hall: A narrow ledge curves around a slick natural shaft. Runes etched into the stone glow faintly when touched. (DC 14 Arcana: Elvish and Draconic prayers of binding). Whispers echo back altered after moments.
  • Pilgrim’s Antechamber: Rectangular room with benches, a broken altar, and six rusted offering bowls (one with a desiccated tongue). Dripping blood into the warm bowl manifests a spectral door leading to an old meditation niche once used by priestesses of Yew.
  • Collapsed Tunnel: Partially blocked eastern passage. Disturbing 10+ lbs of rubble triggers a glyph of warding (4d6 necrotic + 1 min blindness, DC 15 CON). Clearing the tunnel reveals a spiral staircase to Level 1: The Forgotten Reliquary.

Backstory

Once the High Oracle of the Moonspire, Sister Ivoura foresaw her order’s fall and sealed herself inside Hemlock Caverns to guard the Ember Key—a relic capable of unlocking the mountain’s heart and the Dark Gate. Though she died, her spirit remained, half-living, bound to the Caverns by duty and prophecy. She senses Kira’s coming fate and fears it deeply.

Side Quest

Objective:

  • Collect three elemental “tears” from the region:
  • A shard of moonlight from the basin (during full moon)
  • Ash pollen from cursed hemlock bloom on the cliffside (DC 15 Survival)

Reward:
  • Access to the Reliquary of Binding
  • Divine Memory: 1-use spell allows one question to a dead god or ancient hero

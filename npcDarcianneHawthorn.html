NPC: Princess Hawthorn of House Yew – Level 6 Sorcerer (Shadow Magic)

  • Class/Levels: Sorcerer (Shadow Magic – Level 6)
  • Background: Noble (Royal Successor)
  • Alignment: Lawful Neutral (with Chaotic leanings)
  • Age: 28
  • Gender: Female
  • Height: 5’7”
  • Eyes: Violet, intense
  • Hair: Golden-blonde curls braided with silver cords
  • Skin: Pale with a faint dusk-like undertone when casting spells
  • Role: Political Ally, Sorceress, Royal Successor

  • Hidden Scroll Case: Contains forged contract by Varis
  • Diadem with Sapphire: Glows faintly when Darkness is cast
  • Notebook: Political schematics, coded letters, and page from Liora’s spellbook
  • Magic Item – Crown of the Half-Seen:
    • Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement)
    • Advantage on Deception and Insight checks
    • 1/day: Misty Step without spell slot

    • Contains fragment of Adonais’s memory (secret)

Personality & Backstory

  • Trait: "Every word is a dance, every silence a weapon."
  • Ideal: "A just ruler must sometimes wear a liar’s crown."
  • Bond: "If Elira still lives… I will undo the shame I cast on her name."
  • Flaw: "I believe I alone can control the darkness."
  • Player's Goal: Navigate collapsing politics, undo Varis’s leverage, and possibly restore Elira’s name.
  • Background Summary: Trained in diplomacy and shadow magic, Princess Hawthorn ascended as heir after Elira’s disappearance. She is secretly blackmailed by Minister Varis, whom she once allied with to protect the royal family’s secrets. Now, as threats grow and her powers deepen, she must choose between crown and conscience. Liora, her former governess, holds key truths about her origins.

Relationships

  • Elira: Presumed-dead cousin; Hawthorn seeks to redeem her memory
  • Minister Varis: Antagonist and blackmailer with ties to dark elves
  • Liora: Governess and secret-keeper of Hawthorn’s magical bloodline
  • Jorsh: Former lover she once helped escape; his return complicates her loyalties

Locations Associated

  • Royal Castle of House Yew: Seat of power and political turmoil
  • Temple of the Moonspire: Holds the first piece of a mysterious puzzle
  • Hemlock Caverns: Near a shadow crossing that threatens the realm

Roleplaying Notes

  • Speaks with guarded warmth; sarcastic when safe
  • May oppose player goals at first, then align once trust is earned
  • Hides guilt over Elira's fate and fears her own corruption
  • Will sacrifice nearly anything to protect her family’s honor

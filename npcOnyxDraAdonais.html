NPC: Onyx – Hidden Acolyte of the Archons

Basic Information

  • Race: Hill Dwarf
  • Class/Levels: Cleric (Grave Domain) – Level 5
  • Background: Sage (Hidden Acolyte of the Archons)
  • Alignment: Lawful Good (with shades of cosmic Neutrality)
  • Age: ??? (appears ~120)
  • Gender: Male
  • Height: 4’6”
  • Eyes: Cloudy silver
  • Hair: Braided white beard with gold-threaded runes
  • Skin: Earth-brown with faint cracks like stone
  • Role: Ally, Oracle, Guardian of the Grave

  • Languages: Common, Dwarvish, Celestial, Abyssal

Special Traits & Features

  • Circle of Mortality: Spare the Dying becomes a bonus action; heals at max when used on targets with 0 HP.
  • Eyes of the Grave: Detect undead within 60 ft, usable 4 times per long rest.
  • Channel Divinity – Path to the Grave: As an action, curse a creature to become vulnerable to the next attack against it (double damage).

Spellcasting

  • Spellcasting Ability: Wisdom (DC 15, +7 to hit)
  • Spell Slots: 4 (1st), 3 (2nd), 2 (3rd)
  • Cantrips: Spare the Dying (bonus action), Toll the Dead, Light, Guidance
  • 1st Level: Bane*, Shield of Faith
  • 2nd Level: Gentle Repose*, Lesser Restoration
  • 3rd Level: Revivify, Spirit Guardians, Prayer of Healing, Dispel Magic
  • Domain Spells (always prepared): False Life, Bane (1st); Gentle Repose, Ray of Enfeeblement (2nd)

Equipment

  • Hammer of the Radiant Seal: +1 warhammer; glows when undead or fiends are within 30 ft; once per long rest, cast Protection from Evil and Good. If used on a creature cursed by Path to the Grave, deals +1d8 radiant damage on hit.
  • Dwarven Chainmail: Runes of Adonais etched into the steel links
  • Adamantine Shield: Circular, bearing the archonic sigil
  • Celestial Scrolls: Prophecies penned in divine script
  • 2 Healing Potions
  • Pearl of Power: Regain one expended spell slot per day

Personality & Backstory

  • Trait: “I act like a doddering sage. But I remember war in the stars.”
  • Ideal: “True balance requires great sacrifice. I am willing.”
  • Bond: “Kira must be protected. Her bloodline may hold the key to restoring the Gate.”
  • Flaw: “I speak cryptically… because I fear what will happen when I speak plainly.”
  • Player's Goal: To awaken fully as Adonais, protect Kira from the Shadowborn, and stop the rise of Yaldaboath and Varis’s schemes.
  • Background Summary: Onyx, once a humble healer and scribe, reawakened ancient memories of his life as the Archon Adonais after entering the Hemlock Caverns with Baider. That event changed him, driving a wedge between old allies. Now he resides in Belladonna, eccentric and enigmatic, preparing for a rising darkness that only he seems to remember clearly.

Relationships

  • Kira Coldspring: Feels an unshakable duty to protect her; believes she is linked to the divine bloodline or the Shadowbound.
  • Baider: Former friend turned estranged after their journey to the Dark Gate; rift formed due to Onyx’s obsession with prophecy.

Locations Associated

  • Belladonna: Lives as a village elder and eccentric sage.
  • Hemlock Caverns: Site of memory awakening and the sealed Dark Gate.

Roleplaying Notes

  • Refers to divine prophecy as “weather reports.”
  • Often forgets he’s pretending to be mortal.
  • Keeps diagrams of stars and leylines carved under his cottage floorboards.
  • Believes he will need to die again to save the Realm.

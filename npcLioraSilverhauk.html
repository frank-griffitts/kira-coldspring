NPC: Liora Silverhauk – Governess of House Hawthorn

Basic Information

  • Race: Human
  • Class/Levels: Wizard (Divination) Level 7
  • Background: Court Sage
  • Alignment: Lawful Neutral
  • Age: Appears 55 (Actual age closer to 180)
  • Gender: Female
  • Height: Unknown
  • Eyes: Unknown
  • Hair: Unknown
  • Skin: Unknown
  • Role: Royal Advisor, Secret Keeper, Arcane Guardian

Combat Statistics

  • Armor Class (AC): 14 (Mage Armor or enchanted robes)

  • Proficiency Bonus: +3
  • Saving Throws: Intelligence +7, Wisdom +5
  • Skills: Arcana +7, History +7, Insight +5, Persuasion +4
  • Damage Resistances: None
  • Condition Immunities: Sleep (magical), Charm (advantage vs)
  • Senses: Passive Perception 12
  • Languages: Common, Elvish, Draconic, Infernal, Abyssal
  • Challenge Rating (CR): Unknown

Special Traits & Features

  • Arcane Recovery (1/Day): Regain spell slots equal to half wizard level after a short rest.
  • Divination Savant: Halved cost and time for copying divination spells.
  • Portent (2/day): Roll two d20s after a long rest and replace rolls with them.
  • Elven Accuracy: Reroll one d20 when attacking with advantage using Dex, Int, Wis, or Cha.
  • Fey Ancestry: Advantage vs charm, immune to magical sleep.

Spellcasting

  • Spellcasting Ability: Intelligence (Save DC 15, +7 to hit)
  • Spell Slots: 1st (4), 2nd (3), 3rd (3), 4th (1)
  • Cantrips: Prestidigitation, Mage Hand, Ray of Frost, Message
  • 1st Level: Shield, Mage Armor, Detect Magic, Comprehend Languages
  • 2nd Level: Mirror Image, Misty Step, Augury
  • 3rd Level: Counterspell, Dispel Magic, Clairvoyance
  • 4th Level: Arcane Eye

Equipment

  • Wand of Secrets: Detects hidden doors/traps (3 charges/day)
  • Spellbook: Wyrm-hide tome with multilingual coded notes
  • Moonlace Circlet: +1 to spell save DC
  • Courtier’s Robes: Subtle constellation shimmer via minor illusion
  • Ring of Sending: Linked to Princess Hawthorn

Personality & Backstory

  • Trait: "Speaks slowly and with deliberate calm, like one addressing a candle about to blow out."
  • Ideal: "Knowledge is power, and power must be wielded wisely—or not at all."
  • Bond: "She raised Princess Hawthorn like her own."
  • Flaw: "Distrusts prophecy, even though her magic draws upon it."
  • Player's Goal: Liora may serve as a key to understanding Kira’s past and to unlocking sealed knowledge about the Realm of Shadows.
  • Background Summary: Former court mage of the Shadow Realm. Escaped Varis's uprising with a fragment of the Codex N’Thyl. Now she subtly steers the Hawthorn line. Knows Kira’s secret. Keeps a sealed mirror-gate to the Realm of Shadows in her hidden chamber.

Relationships

  • Princess Hawthorn: Raised her from birth. Shares a deep, maternal bond.
  • Varis: Former ally turned enemy after betrayal in the Shadow Realm.
  • Kira Coldspring: Knows a hidden truth about Kira’s past.
  • Baider: Knows him well enough to trust his judgment despite his gruffness.
  • Rellan Thornwatch: A respectful rivalry over how fate should be interpreted.

Locations Associated

  • Royal Castle of House Hawthorn: Governess and royal advisor.
  • Temple of the Moonspire: Knows of a failing magical seal.
  • Belladonna Archives: Frequent researcher and watcher of forbidden texts.
  • Realm of Shadows: Escaped from here; keeps a sealed mirror-gate to it.

Roleplaying Notes

  • Speaks with deliberate grace; uses pauses for emphasis.
  • Rarely reveals the full truth unless absolutely necessary.
  • Has layers of old grief, hope, and protective instincts toward the royal family.

Plot Hooks

  • Liora needs help unsealing the mirror-gate to the Realm of Shadows.
  • She may task the party with guarding the Princess during an upcoming eclipse.
  • Can reveal Kira's hidden lineage during a moment of crisis.
  • Might request the retrieval of a lost page of the Codex N’Thyl from Hemlock Caverns.
  • Opposes Varis's quiet coup — and needs eyes within the court.

DUNGEON MASTERS ONLY

Location: Sumacwick Ruins – Mercenary Camp

Exterior Description

Nestled in the shadow of ancient, crumbling ziggurats, the camp is surrounded by skeletal trees and low-lying fog. Broken columns jut from the earth like jagged teeth, remnants of a civilization long devoured by time. Ramshackle tents and iron-banded wagons form a loose horseshoe shape around a roaring fire pit where a spit roasts some unfortunate forest beast. Banners with false heraldry flap in the wind—ostensibly a freelance company called the Iron Vultures—but subtle sigils of the Ministry are worked into the stitching (DC 15 Investigation to detect). Lanterns glow with green flame, powered by alchemical oil imported from Belladonna.

Interior Description

  • Command Tent: Reinforced with enchanted canvas and warded by a silent alarm glyph. Contains tactical maps of the Hemlock Caverns, trade routes, and troop markers. A chest holds coded letters from Minister Varis (DC 16 to decode).
  • Mercenary Quarters: Eight reinforced tents house 2–3 hardened sellswords each. Inside are cots, weapon racks, and alchemy kits. A rumor board in the largest tent displays targets of interest: Kira Coldspring, Elias, and a veiled portrait of Princess Hawthorn.
  • Prison Cages: Wooden and iron cages beneath a canvas overhang. Currently imprisoned: a local scout who overheard Ministry orders, and a wounded drow emissary suspected of betrayal. One cage is booby-trapped with a glyph of necrotic discharge (2d8 necrotic, DC 15).
  • Supply Wagon (Hidden): Contains stolen relics from the Temple of the Moonspire. A hidden compartment houses Aether-bond shackles and Dark Elf communication stones.

Backstory

Veyra was once a royal guard in a distant mountain kingdom, exiled for executing a corrupt noble without trial. Recruited by Minister Varis, she serves not for coin but for the promise of political revenge and a homeland to rule. She suspects Varis is hiding something about the real purpose of their excavation at Hemlock Ruins—but her honor is entangled with blood and ambition. She quietly respects Kira’s prowess and would rather recruit her than kill her.

Hidden Chamber / Puzzle / Treasure

Location: Beneath the Ziggurat Ruins – The Hall of Echoes
Entry: A spiral staircase under a stone slab leads to a sealed chamber used for summoning rituals.
Puzzle:

  • Four pedestals emit distinct musical tones
  • Recreate the “song of silence” used to imprison demons
  • INT (Arcana) DC 16 to determine order
  • WIS (Insight) DC 18 to sense the disharmonious pedestal
  • Failure summons a Shadowbound Horror
Treasure:
  • Sigil of Silence: 1/long rest: silence a room and dispel illusions
  • Obsidian Coin of Varis: Can be used to track or eavesdrop on Ministry agents

Map of the Kingdom of Yew

Kingdom of Yew

This kingdom is home to several important locations, including the town of Belladonna and the Hawthorn Royal Palace.

  • Belladonna: A central township with various key sites.
    • Magistrate's Hall: Where Magistrate Corvin Dellmar Pinkerton presides.
    • Town Square: Home to Kira Coldspring and Mira Vensh.
    • Wymblood Flask: A popular tavern.
    • Smithy: Baider’s Blacksmith Shop.
    • Rangers Guild: Led by Warden Rellen (Elira Hawthorn); site of past betrayals.
    • Ranger Apartment District (Quiver Row): Home to Kira, Elias, and Dardick Hawk.
    • Whispering Well: Monitored secretly by the Magistrate.
  • Hawthorn Royal Palace: The center of royal power.
    • Library: Sacred archive overseen by Liora.
    • Ministry of Trade: Secretly run by Minister Varis.

Wilderness and Other Regions of Yew

  • Blackpines Outpost: An abandoned outpost in a cursed thicket.
  • Mercenary Camp: Secretly Ministry-aligned, led by Veyra Stonebrand.
  • Hemlock Falls: Waterfall masking the entrance to Hemlock Caverns.
  • Lost Reliquary: Level 1 – Sanctum of divine relics.
  • Catacombs of Echoes: Level 2 – Corrupted burial site.
  • Draconis Incubatorium: Level 2 – Defiled dragon hatchery.
  • Chamber of Portals: Level 3 – The Dark Gate and planar seal.
  • Hawthorn’s Retreat: Princess Hawthorn's hidden redoubt.
  • Moonspire Temple: Ruins honoring Astaroth the Terrible.
  • Onyx’s Cottage: A disguised celestial outpost.

Shadow Realm (Umbra'Thal)

  • Hanging City: The capital metropolis beneath the world.
  • Royal Citadel: Palace of Mirrors, seat of Queen Morvelya.
  • Mirror Throne Hall: Obsidian chamber above the shadow-rift.
  • Penumbra Sanctum: Morvelya’s private magical sanctum.
  • Soul Vaults: Crypt for soul-bound prisoners, including Lord Althion Coldspring.
  • Shadow Cistern: Vaulted tunnels beneath Umbra'Thal.
  • Dreamcairn Quarter: District of memory and longing.
  • Coldspring House: Ancestral home of Kira.
  • House of Whispers: Spire-temple of the Unspeaking Order.
  • Village of Creosote: Fungal village of bone-white towers.

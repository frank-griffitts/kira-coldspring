DUNGEON MASTERS ONLY

Location: Level 3 – The Dark Gate

Exterior Description

Deep beneath the Catacombs of Echoes lies a scorched staircase of obsidian and bone, revealed only when the Echo Prism meets radiant light. The descent flickers with eldritch whispers, each step naming forgotten souls. Divine lights falter. Torches fail. A -1 penalty applies to all saving throws against fear while in the Dark Gate. At its end stands an immense door, pulsing red beneath six radiant glyphs. One glyph has already gone dark.

Interior Description

  • The Antechamber of Sins: Six statues represent Pride, Wrath, Envy, Sloth, Lust, and Despair. Each triggers a roleplay test of virtue. Four must be passed to deactivate the seal. Failures spawn a Shadow of Sin. Examples:
    • Wrath: Burn something precious to move forward.
    • Despair: Relive your greatest failure, and choose to stand again.
    • Lust: A ghostly lover tempts a PC to forsake the quest for bliss.
  • The Heartlock Chamber: At the Gate's base sits a molten pit and three Archon Seals. Only Adonais or marked descendants can affect them. Inside lies the Heartlock—a crystal prison for Yaldaboath’s essence. It can be destroyed for power or sealed at great cost.
  • The Opened Gate: A broken realm of fog and low stars. Physics unravel. The Herald of Yaldaboath awaits.

Backstory

The Dark Gate was forged by the Archon Adonais at the dawn of mortal time—not just to repel Yaldaboath but to imprison part of him in the Material Plane. Bound by mortal faith, the seal has faded as belief withers. One glyph has gone dark. If all six fail, a second Sundering will begin.

Side Quest

“Shatter or Seal”
Objective:

  • Decide whether to destroy the Heartlock or restore the seal.
If Restored:
  • A sacrifice is required—Onyx or a PC must give up powers or life
  • Divine Intervention possible
  • Permanent boons from the Archon Host
If Shattered:
  • One PC gains a Mythic Boon (+2 stat, new subclass or spell list)
  • Planar anomalies begin across Yew
  • Varis advances his summoning schemes

Hidden Chamber / Puzzle / Treasure

Puzzle: Behind the Heartlock pedestal lies the Celestial Dial, a rotating ring with six glyphs. Aligning them properly (DC 18 Religion) reveals a hidden vault.
Treasure:

  • Vowstone of Adonais: One-time resurrection of any creature—even those beyond resurrection. If misused, user gains Echo Madness.

Monsters

  • Sin Infusion (Recharge 6): Inflicts a “Sin Curse” (e.g., Wrath = WIS save disadvantage)
